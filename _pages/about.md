I am a Ph.D. student in the [Department of Statistics](https://artsci.tamu.edu/statistics/index.html) at Texas A&M University, where I have the fortune of being advised by Dr. [Suhasini Subba Rao](https://sites.google.com/view/suhasinisubbarao/home?authuser=0) and am primarily focused on time-series analysis. As a recipient of [Hagler Fellowship](https://hias.tamu.edu/faculty-fellows/), I have a previlege of working with Dr. [Alicia Carriquiry](https://artsci.tamu.edu/statistics/contact/profiles/alicia-carriquiry.html) on applying statistical models to various fields, including nutrition. Prior to joining Texas A&M, I was working with Dr. [Jungsoon Choi](https://sites.google.com/hanyang.ac.kr/jschoi) at Hanyang University in Seoul, Korea on spatio-temporal modeling.

My research interests mainly include
- time-series analysis,
- graphical models for time-series,
- nonstationary process,
- and fourier analysis on spatio-temporal data.
